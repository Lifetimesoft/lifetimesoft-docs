# Add Identity Account

How to add an Identity Account such as a phone number or email to the system.

## Steps

### 1. Click the Add button in the Identity Account section

![add-btn](https://static.lifetimesoft.com/docs/img/watch-add-btn.png)

### 2. Select the Type, fill in the data accordingly, then click Add Data

![add-identity-account](https://static.lifetimesoft.com/docs/img/add-identity-account.png)
