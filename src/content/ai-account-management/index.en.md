# AI Account Management

AI Account Management is a system for managing AI accounts used within the LifeTimeSoft platform. This allows you to securely add, view, and manage your AI provider accounts (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, etc.) in one place.

## Features

- **View Accounts**: List all connected AI accounts and their current status.
- **Add Account**: Connect new AI accounts to the system by providing necessary API keys and credentials.
- **Manage Permissions**: Configure which services or agents can access specific AI accounts.

## Getting Started

To get started, you can:
1. View your current accounts by navigating to the AI Account Management dashboard.
2. Click on **Add Account** to connect a new AI provider.
