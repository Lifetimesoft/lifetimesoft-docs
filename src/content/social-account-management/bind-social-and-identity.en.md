# Link Social Account with Identity Account

This guide explains how to link a Social Account with an Identity Account, allowing you to connect your social media account with a specific identity in the system.

This helps you verify which Identity is associated with each Social Media account.

## Steps

### 1. Select the Social Media account

Click on the Social Media account you want to link with an Identity. The system will redirect you to the **Social Media Management** page.

### 2. Click Edit to link the account

There are two sections: **Identity Mail** and **Identity Tel**.  
On the right side, click the **pencil (edit) icon** to modify and link the account.

### 3. Choose the Identity to link

After clicking edit, the system will display a list of available Identities associated with the user.  
Select the Identity you want to link with the Social Account.

### 4. Save changes

Once you’ve selected the desired Identity, click **Save** to confirm the linkage.
